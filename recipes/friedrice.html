Fried Rice

Description

Fried rice is a versatile and popular dish in many Asian cuisines, known for its delightful combination of flavors and textures. This dish typically involves cooking pre-cooked rice in a wok or skillet, along with a variety of ingredients such as diced vegetables, proteins like meat or tofu, and seasonings like soy sauce and aromatics. The rice is stir-fried until it absorbs the flavors of the other ingredients and takes on a slightly crispy texture. The dish is often enhanced with the addition of ingredients like scrambled eggs, green onions, and a blend of savory spices. Fried rice offers a satisfying and comforting meal that can be customized with different ingredients to suit individual preferences, making it a beloved and widely enjoyed dish around the world.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Vegetables
  • Protein
  • Soy Sauce
  • Eggs

Cooking Steps

  1. Prepare ingredients and cook rice
  2. Sautee aromatics and cook protein
  3. Add vegetables to wok
  4. Stir in rice and soy sauce
  5. Add scrambled egg and garnish
